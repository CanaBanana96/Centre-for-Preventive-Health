Healthy Pune Initiative

"Healthy Kids, Healthy Family, Healthy Nation" - Our flagship community outreach program

Program Overview

The Healthy Pune Initiative is our flagship outreach program in collaboration with DISHA Foundation, aimed at preventing and reversing lifestyle diseases at the community level.

Through structured health education and lifestyle interventions, we're creating healthier communities across Pune. Our program focuses on evidence-based approaches to tackle the growing NCD epidemic through grassroots engagement.

Program Components

Four key outreach programs designed to reach different segments of the community

School Outreach Program

Health education and lifestyle awareness for students, parents, and teachers

  • Interactive health workshops
  • Nutrition education for children
  • Physical activity programs
  • Parent-teacher health seminars

Residential Societies Outreach

Health talks, lifestyle screening camps, and on-site counselling

  • Health awareness sessions
  • Free health screenings
  • Lifestyle counseling
  • Community cooking workshops

Corporate Outreach

Employee wellness programs focused on stress, sleep, diet, and metabolic health

  • Workplace wellness seminars
  • Stress management workshops
  • Ergonomic assessments
  • Health screening camps

Community Health Camps

Health camps and digital campaigns across urban neighbourhoods

  • Free health check-ups
  • Health education drives
  • Social media awareness
  • Community health fairs
